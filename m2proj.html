    Cat Facts

    Cat Tracks

    Cats walk by placing their back paws exactly into the footprints created by their front paws. This movement is called direct registering meaning cats can be very stealthy, as they make fewer tracks and less noise when walking.

    According to Scientific American, cats are the only mammals that are lacking the genes to taste sweetness, meaning they probably don't get to enjoy sugars or experience a sugar rush!

    The Purrrfect Sound

    Purring actually improves bone density and promotes healing within a cat. The purring frequency, 26 Hertz, also aides in tissue regeneration and can help stimulate the repair of weak and brittle bones.

    Salt Savvy

    Unlike humans, cats can safely drink seawater! This is because cats have kidneys that can filter out the high levels of salt and use the remaining water for hydration.
